Yaerim Gen Kwon is a prose writer from Seoul, South Korea. She currently resides in Bloomington, Indiana, where she teaches and studies fiction at Indiana University and serves as the Editor-in-Chief for Indiana Review.

Her short story won first prize for Literature in 2022 and her novel chapter received the Hegarty Award in 2023 from the National Society of Arts & Letters Competition. She received scholarships and fellowships from AWP, Carleton College, Indiana University, and New York State Summer Writers Institute. In her former life, she worked in media in Seoul, San Francisco, and Rabat. She is working on her debut novel titled Upward Mobility.
